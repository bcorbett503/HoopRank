Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download HoopRank from the App Store and sign in using your Google or Facebook account, or use email/password login.

How do I rate other players?

After playing a game with someone, go to their profile and tap "Rate Player". Rate their skills in categories like shooting, defense, and court vision.

How are rankings calculated?

Rankings are based on an aggregate of all ratings you receive from other players. The more games you play and get rated, the more accurate your ranking becomes.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting us at support@hooprank.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

Is HoopRank free?

Yes! HoopRank is completely free to download and use.
